OUR STORY:

This is a place where we share our passion and power as a black youth owned entity.We bring the season's best mix of organic and local produce together to develop a unique menu at extremely affordable prices for every kind of palete. We are determined to provide healthy and especially mouthwatering meals to all,be it the young or the old.Our one wish is to help our delicious food find a good home.YOUR HOME.With being a mobile entity,we also provide delivery at a minimum fee.
WHY WE ARE DIFFERENT?
To compete in the mobile kitchen business, it’s important to be accessible to our customer base with real-time location menu information. High traffic locations with professional customers who have little to no food options during short lunch breaks are a big priority. Establishing a good relationship with customers is important. This relationship will be based on excellent service. When compared to other mobile kitchens Hungry Dealers will feature a simple but quality menu, local grown vegetables from local vegetable distributors. We also have the support of Bright On Charcoal. An upcoming charcoal manufacturing and distribution. So we have charcoal as our backup if anything should go wrong.
